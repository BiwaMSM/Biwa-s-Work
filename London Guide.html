London Guide

London is the capital and largest city of England and the United Kingdom, with a population of around 8.8 million.

North London

North London is the northern part of London, England, north of the River Thames. It extends from Clerkenwell and Finsbury, on the edge of the City of London financial district, to Greater London's boundary with Hertfordshire.

East London

East London is the northeastern part of London, England, east of the ancient City of London and north of the River Thames as it begins to widen.

South London

South London is the southern part of London, England, south of the River Thames.

West London

West London is the western part of London, England, north of the River Thames, west of the City of London, and extending to the Greater London boundary.
